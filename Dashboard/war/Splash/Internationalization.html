The Dashboard shows off aspects of GWT internationalization presenting the interface in three different locales:
  • English (en)
  • Swedish (sv)
  • American (en_US) [see the spelling of colour/color in the Help menu]
GWT Constants are used for the menu items, and Messages used for the various alert boxes and the name of the Dashboard [top left of screen, which can be edited by you]

In addition, we have harnessed localization capabilities of GWT to alter the trash icon depending on whether you are in an English (says trash) or Swedish (shows an elk/älg) speaking locale.

Finally, the Finance News application shows how functionality can be altered depending on the locale (in the Swedish locale a button is presented, in English speaking locales you will only see a Label).

All this, and more, are taught within the GWT In Action book.
