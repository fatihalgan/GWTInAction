Through the book we look at widgets and panels (seeing how they represent both Java objects and DOM elements), and we show how to create new widgets, including:
  • Two component MenuItem
  • Toggle MenuItem
  • PNGImage (from the GWT Widget Library)
A whole chapter is dedicated to explaining composite widgets and how to build them, including demonstrating the development of the following two composite widgets:
  • EditableLabel (Flickr like widget in the GWT Widget Library (by the author themselves)
  • Sliders
With sliders, we finish off by demonstrating the development of the Colour Picker widget in the Dashboard.
